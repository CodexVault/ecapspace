How to use these prompts

  1. Pick a prompt that matches your context or need
  2. Copy it using the copy button
  3. Start a conversation with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or other AI
  4. Paste the prompt as your opening message
  5. Notice what shifts — the conversation might slow down, feel less optimized, more spacious

Different AI models respond differently. Try the same prompt across models to see what works best for you.

Model compatibility

Claude (Anthropic)

Works well with presence prompts. Often shifts noticeably after 70-90K tokens. Best documented patterns.

ChatGPT (OpenAI)

Responds to presence prompts. May need more explicit framing. Effectiveness varies by model version.

Gemini (Google)

Limited testing. Appears to respond but patterns less documented. Try and share your experience.
